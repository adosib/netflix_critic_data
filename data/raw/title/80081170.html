    24m

    When Sheriff Cannonball takes off with the Rabble Rouser, Buddy takes over his job. Buddy thinks his truck is talking to him.

    24m

    Buddy becomes a repo man so he can get his stuff back from Big Tex. Big Tex steals Buddy's driving secrets, but Buddy has a plan for revenge.

  4. 4. Thunder Fu / Funny Money

    24m

    24m

    24m

    Moneybags finally gets his chance to race Buddy and tries to trick Darnell into helping him win the race -- and take over the whole town of Greasepit.
