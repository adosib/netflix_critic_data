Episodes

Dirty Jobs

Release year: 2005

Host Mike Rowe rolls up his sleeves and gets down and dirty on this series that explores all the toughest, grungiest jobs that someone just has to do.

  1. 1. Bat Cave Scavenger

    43m

    Fearless host Mike braves heat, humidity and beetles while gathering guano in a Texas cave, before collecting mud for baseballs and fileting fish.

  2. 2. Worm Dung Farmer

    43m

    Mike gets a crash course in "catfish noodling," emptying septic tanks and feeding assorted creepy-crawlies at a Florida worm ranch.

  3. 3. Roadkill Cleaners

    43m

    Collecting lost golf balls from terrifying waters, facilitating horse breeding and cleaning up roadkill are all on the menu for Mike.

  4. 4. Chinatown Garbage Collector

    42m

    Mike does his part for the environment in San Francisco's Chinatown, assists with shark tagging and hunts for useful car parts in a muddy junkyard.

  5. 5. Sewer Inspector

    41m

    Mike steps into the shoes of a sewer inspector in San Francisco and enlists a disaster cleanup crew after a Brooklyn toilet explodes from a sewer backup. He then joins up with a demolition company, where he works as part of a deconstruction crew.

  6. 6. Pig Farmer

    42m

    Mike Rowe steps into the shoes of an Iowa pig farmer, where he faces the challenges of feeding and cleaning up after hundreds of dirty pigs. Then, Mike learns how to care for a new litter of piglets and artificially inseminates a female pig.

  7. 7. Chick Sexer

    42m

    Differentiating sexes at a chicken hatchery proves difficult for Mike, while brewing beer and harvesting oysters bring their own nasty challenges.

  8. 8. Vexcon

    42m

    Mike takes to the high seas to catch some king crab. Other thankless tasks include making horseshoes and shadowing exterminators facing an infestation.

  9. 9. Sludge Cleaner

    42m

    Hang ten! Mike attempts to craft a surfboard, extract honey from a buzzing beehive and clean up thick sludge beneath a New York bus depot.

  10. 10. Hot Tar Roofer

    42m

    Hot-tar roofing under the California sun puts Mike's stamina to the test — as does helping to move an entire house for a historical society.

  11. 11. Ostrich Farmer

    42m

    Join Mike Rowe as he rolls up his sleeves and gets dirty on a Hawaiian coffee bean plantation, learns how to nurse an injured sea lion pup back to health, and works as an ostrich farmer in the California desert.

  12. 12. Cheese Maker

    42m

    Which job is messier? Cleaning enclosures at San Francisco Zoo, churning milk for cheese or gathering volcanic ash for mud baths? Mike finds out.

  13. 13. Shrimper

    42m

    Mike joins a hardy crew to check the shrimp population, explores a massive swamp in search of crawfish and learns how to recycle tires.

  14. 14. Bio-Diesel-Man

    42m

    Building a cob house from soil, clay and straw gets Mike nice and dirty. He also evacuates a beehive from a home and makes fuel from vegetable oil.

  15. 15. Micro Algae Man

    42m

    Things get hairy when Mike shadows a pet groomer. Other tasks include collecting goopy algae at a facility in Hawaii and sorting and bagging charcoal.

  16. 16. Chimney Sweeper

    42m

    Mike tries his luck as a chimney sweep, salvages precious wood from watery depths to make furniture and chops up scrap metal at a recycling plant.

  17. 17. Avian Vomitologist

    42m

    Mike heads deep into a marble quarry and learns how ancient sculptors got their building blocks. He then harvests owl vomit and gets close to a cow.

  18. 18. Turkey Farmer

    42m

    Mike travels to Texas to learn about turning turkeys into compost. His next stops: a Colorado potato farm and a wastewater treatment plant in California.

  19. 19. Alligator Farmer

    42m

    In Louisiana, Mike gets a bitter taste of hard work at a sugar mill. He also assists some firefighters and wrestles a pen full of alligators.

  20. 20. Mushroom Farmer

    42m

    Mike braves some strong odors as he learns about growing mushrooms before tackling some underground clean-up. Then things heat up at a drain factory.

  21. 21. Plumber

    42m

    Mike discovers what else a catfish noodler can do. Then he gets serious about the art of making mud and faces the risks of roughnecking on a drill site.

  22. 22. Termite Controller

    42m

    Mike helps build a street in Texas and exterminates termites in a Louisiana church before finding out just how hard it is to clean a concrete mixer.

  23. 23. Casino Food Recycler

    42m

    Mike Rowe rolls up his sleeves and gets dirty in Las Vegas as he goes behind the scenes of the world-famous Lance Burton Magic Show. Mike also joins forces with a Las Vegas pig farmer who has found an ingenious way to recycle leftovers.

  24. 24. Parade Float Dismantler

    42m

    Mike explores Pittsburgh's steel-town roots at a plant that turns coal into coke. Later, he gets an oyster-shucking lesson and takes apart parade floats.

  25. 25. Garbage Pit Technician

    42m

    Mike becomes the subject of his own limited edition lithographs. Next up: making terracotta from old sewer pipes and turning garbage into power.

  26. 26. Mike's Day Off - Special

    42m

    As Mike takes a rare day off for a thorough medical checkup, some of his bosses shift to his side of the camera to tackle messy gigs.

  27. 27. Skull Cleaner

    42m

    Mike works on the railroad before serving up some local cuisine at a Cajun country kitchen. But cleaning animal skulls poses the toughest challenge.

  28. 28. Geoduck Farmer

    42m

    Mike breaks up a barrier to help protect an endangered trout species. He also cleans an antique pipe organ and farms giant clams known as geoducks.

  29. 29. Fuel Tank Cleaner

    42m

    Mike Rowe joins forces with the Air Force as he travels to McConnell Air Force Base in Wichita, Kan., to clean the Stratotanker fuel tanks. He also learns to make some messy candy and to retread tires.

  30. 30. Coal Miner

    42m

    Mike Rowe gets dirty as he helps a group of New Jersey salvage experts tear apart a dormitory at an abandoned college. Then, Mike rolls up his sleeves and goes to work as a coal miner.

  31. 31. Hoof Cleaner

    42m

    Mike turns big rocks into smaller ones at a quarry in Washington, cleans out some hippopotamus tanks and learns how to give a cow a pedicure.

  32. 32. Alpaca Shearer

    42m

    Mike Rowe joins a crew of filthy farmers on their job at Taylor Shellfish Farms, gets down and dirty in the tropics as he farms taro, and finds out that animals don't like haircuts when he joins a family of alpaca farmers to shear their alpacas.

  33. 33. Monkey Caretaker

    42m

    Mike Rowe meets his match when he travels to South Africa to train monkeys to run wild.

  34. 34. 100th Dirty Job Special

    84m

    Mike Rowe introduces his work with the U.S. Army, sharing stories from behind the scenes and answering viewer mail as he unites America with the dirtiest jobs and the funniest moments of the show.

Release year: 2006

This series celebrates American workers who make a living doing disgusting – but indispensable – jobs.

  1. 1. Snake Researcher

    41m

    Mike goes to Lake Erie to protect endangered water snakes. He removes algae from Lake Michigan.

  2. 2. Penguin Keeper

    42m

    Penguins are cute but they produce stinking manure. Mike realizes this when he works in the Audubon Zoo in New Orleans.

  3. 3. Bell Maker

    42m

    Mike goes to a clock factory to see how horse manure is used in production. And he helps the US army with the cleaning of sea walls.

  4. 4. Mosquito Control Officer

    42m

    New Orleans has been infested with mosquitoes. Mike is hired to trap the mosquitoes by letting out thousands of fish in deserted swimming pools.

  5. 5. Bug Breeder

    42m

    Mike learns how to breed rare and exotic insects. In Tennessee, he helps to reanimate the famous fainting goats.

  6. 6. Poo Pot Maker

    42m

    Mike Rowe is working at a farm to make flower pots out of cow manure. In California, he inspects the salmon population.

  7. 7. Mule Logger

    42m

    Mike digs tunnels which serve as wine cellars. And he meets loggers who have trees towed away by mules.

  8. 8. Well Digger

    42m

    Mike is looking for geothermal water! He realizes how dirty this job is when he is sprayed with mud.

  9. 9. Cave Digger

    42m

    Mike digs wine caves in Napa Valley in California, and then he has to burn waste at Kansas Airport.

  10. 10. Salt Miner

    42m

    Mike works in a salt mine. Working in the claustrophobic spaces with the explosives, he respects the people who put salt on our table.

  11. 11. Wine Maker

    42m

    Mike is in the Napa Valley to turn grapes into wine. He travels to Kansas to breed cattle.

  12. 12. Steam Ship Cleaner

    42m

    Mike learns the art of injection seeding with two men from San Diego. After that, he works on a steam ferry that is more than 100 years old.

  13. 13. Billboard Installer

    42m

    Mike Rowe works with a billboard installer in Texas and learns the intricacies of this dirty job.

  14. 14. Snake Wrangler

    42m

    Mike works in Texas on a snake farm where there are some of the most dangerous snakes! It's a dirty job and Mike's scared to death!

  15. 15. Leather Tanner

    42m

    Mike learns how to tan leather and how cow's leather is made into suede. After that, he sees how artistic jars are made.

  16. 16. Bridge Painter

    42m

    Mike faces wind gusts of 80 km an hour when working on a bridge in Michigan. And he works on a crawler transporter at Kennedy Space Center.

  17. 17. Vomit Island Workers

    42m

    Mike is on Vomit Island to ring and count herons. Then he works at a fish farm in the Mojave desert in California.

  18. 18. Alligator Egg Collector

    42m

    Mike wanders through the swamps of Louisiana in search of 90,000 alligator eggs to save American alligators from extinction.

  19. 19. Wild Goose Chase

    42m

    Mike Rowe travels to the tundra and experiences how a team in a plane herds together wild geese in flight.

  20. 20. Special Effects Artist

    42m

    Mike learns about special effects and is turned into a zombie. And he helps to harvest kelp.

  21. 21. Reef Ball Maker

    42m

    Mike makes reef balls to boost the fish and coral populations in polluted areas. And he makes soap out of goat's milk and repairs water tanks.

  22. 22. Exotic Animal Keeper

    42m

    Mike goes to a recycle center to clean dump trucks. He cleans cages in a rescue for exotic animals and meets a bear marten.

  23. 23. Spray Insulation Technician

    42m

    Mike learns how to paint runways; he studies dirty diapers for dirty scents and works at a spray insulation.

  24. 24. Big Animal Vet

    42m

    Mike Rowe gets his hands dirty in a famous barbecue restaurant, helps a vet with big animals and digs up oil tanks.
